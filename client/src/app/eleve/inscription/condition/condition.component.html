A student can cancel his/her contract anytime and for any reason. Cancellation fees will be applied. The Driving School must reimburse the student for paid and uncompleted courses. Contract expiration and deadline

The contract lasts 18 months. Normally, after 12 months of classes, the student is supposed to finish all his/her classes. If the program has not been completed within the 18 months of contract, the student will have to renew his contract to continue his courses. An amount of $ 120 will be required for the renewal and the price of the courses to be completed will be calculated according to the regular price in effect.

Course certificates

At the end of the program in phase 1 or in case of cancellation of the contract, PCONDUITE will provide you with a course certificate for free. Be careful: if you lose this certificate, you must pay $ 50 to get another one. I have read and understood the terms and conditions.
